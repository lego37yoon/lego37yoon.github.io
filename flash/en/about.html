What is team.fLASH?

2017, 22th Ilsan Daejin High School(Korea) students made a club for game developing. In the progress of making club, two members were joined so there is 5 members in our team.

The origin of our team name is Adobe Flash™, because Adobe Flash™ Platform was one of the successful case of game that supports both web and application.
